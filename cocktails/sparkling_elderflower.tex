\section{Sparkling Elderflower}
\begin{cocktail}
	\yieldsource{1 cocktail}{Empress 1908}{}

	\pre{This spring spritz is a favorite for garden parties and afternoon happy hours alike. If you're a fan of floral flavors, this cocktail is for you!}

	\ingredientlist{
		2 \oz Empress 1908 gin

		\sfrac{1}{2} \oz lime juice

		\sfrac{1}{2} \oz elderflower liqueur

		5 mint leaves

		elderflower soda/tonic (optical)
	}

Shake the gin, lime juice, elderflower liqueur and mint leaves on ice. Strain into a chilled red wine glass. Top with elderflower soda or tonic and garnish with a lime wheel or mint spring.
\end{cocktail}
