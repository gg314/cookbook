\section{Orange Ricotta Pancakes}
\begin{recipe}
	\yieldsourcetime{12 pancakes}{Taste of Home}{30 minutes}

	\pre{The ricotta in this recipe only adds to the texture, but the citrus adds a fresh taste to the pancakes. ``For a different twist, switch the citrus ingredient to lime or lemon juice.''}

	\ingredientlist{
		1 \sfrac{1}{2} \cup all-purpose flour

		3 \T sugar

		1 \sfrac{1}{2} \t baking soda

		\sfrac{1}{4} \t salt

		1 large egg

		1 \cup part-skim ricotta cheese

		\sfrac{3}{4} \cup 2\% milk

		\sfrac{1}{2} \t grated orange zest

		\sfrac{1}{2} \cup orange juice

		\sfrac{1}{4} \cup butter, melted

		\sfrac{1}{2} \t vanilla extract

		maple syrup or powdered sugar, opt.
	}




	In a bowl, whisk the first five ingredients. In another bowl, whisk egg, ricotta, milk, orange zest, orange juice, melted butter and vanilla until blended. Add to dry ingredients; stir just until moistened.

	Lightly grease a griddle; heat over medium heat. Pour batter by 1/4 cupfuls onto griddle. Cook until bubbles on top begin to pop and bottoms are golden brown. Turn; cook until second side is golden brown. Serve with syrup and confectioners' sugar.




\end{recipe}
